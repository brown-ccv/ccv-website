<ContentSection title="How We Support Brown">

We're here to help you navigate our services and make your research computing as seamless as possible. We provide
support for our High-Performance Computing cluster, [Oscar](/services/oscar), our secure compute and storage system,
[Stronghold](/services/stronghold), various [storage and transfer services](/services/storage) like Globus and
Hibernate, and more!

We can assist with:

- **Consulting** on which of CCV systems best fits your research needs.
- **Onboarding** to CCV systems.
- **Troubleshooting** and debugging your issues and problems when using CCV systems.

</ContentSection>

<ContentSection title="How to Get Help">
We offer a variety of support options designed to fit your needs. Choose the method that works best for you.

### Documentation & Self-Service

Our comprehensive <a href="https://docs.ccv.brown.edu/documentation/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">documentation</a> is the fastest way to find answers to common questions about using our systems.

### Workshops & Tutorials

- Join our **weekly online HPC tutorials** during the spring and fall semesters. Please check out our
<a href="https://events.brown.edu/ccv/all" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Events Schedule</a> for upcoming sessions.
- <a href="https://docs.ccv.brown.edu/bootcamp/bootcamp-2025" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">CCV Bootcamp</a>
combines our weekly tutorials in a condensed format each June. All sessions are recorded and available for you to watch
at your convenience on our bootcamp website.

### Direct Support

We have many avenues to support you directly. Please see information on office hours, opening a ticket, and our
community Slack on our Contact Us page.
